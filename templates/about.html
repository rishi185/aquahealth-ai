About AquaHealth

We're on a mission to revolutionize aquaculture through artificial intelligence. Our platform empowers fish farmers and aquaculture professionals with real-time disease detection, water quality monitoring, and predictive analytics.

By combining deep learning models with IoT sensor technology, we aim to reduce fish mortality rates, optimize farm operations, and contribute to global food security.
